Inktober 2017

An inktober is a world wide event, in which you make a drawing in ink for every day of October. I participated in this event for the first time in 2017 with good results

I didn't manage to make a drawing for each of the days, but the ones I did I was very happy how they came out. On each day a word was given, which you could use for inspiration to your ink drawing. You can find the rest of the drawings below here.
